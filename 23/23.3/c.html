$$ \begin {split} g\left(x\right) &= \lfloor x \rfloor + \lfloor x + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \end {split} $$

(c)

推导:
令 $ x = i + \sigma \quad i \in Z, 0\leq \sigma < 1 $ $$ \begin {split} g\left(x\right) &= \lfloor x \rfloor + \lfloor x + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= \lfloor i + \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor i + \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= i + \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + i + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ \end {split} \tag{1} $$ $$ \begin {split} \lfloor 2 \cdot x \rfloor &= \lfloor 2 \cdot (i + \sigma) \rfloor \\ &= \lfloor 2 \cdot i + 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor \\ \end {split} \tag{2} $$ 我们发现$\lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor = \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor$因为 $$ \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor = \begin{cases}0 \quad\quad 当 0 \leq \sigma < \dfrac{1}{2} \\1 \quad\quad 当 \dfrac{1}{2} \leq \sigma < 1 \end{cases} $$ 而 $$ \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor = \begin{cases}0 \quad\quad 当 0 \leq \sigma < \dfrac{1}{2} \\1 \quad\quad 当 \dfrac{1}{2} \leq \sigma < 1 \end{cases} $$ 所以$(1) = (2)$命题得证
