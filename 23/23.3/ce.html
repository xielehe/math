推导:
$$ \begin {split} g\left(x\right) &= \lfloor x \rfloor + \lfloor x + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= \lfloor i + \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor i + \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= i + \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + i + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor \\ \end {split} \tag{1} $$ $$ \begin {split} \lfloor 2 \cdot x \rfloor &= \lfloor 2 \cdot (i + \sigma) \rfloor \\ &= \lfloor 2 \cdot i + 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor \\ &= 2 \cdot i + \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor \\ \end {split} \tag{2} $$ 我们发现$\lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor = \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor$因为 $$ \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{2} \rfloor = \begin{cases}0 \quad\quad 当 0 \leq \sigma < \dfrac{1}{2} \\1 \quad\quad 当 \dfrac{1}{2} \leq \sigma < 1 \end{cases} $$ 而 $$ \lfloor 2 \cdot \sigma \rfloor = \begin{cases}0 \quad\quad 当 0 \leq \sigma < \dfrac{1}{2} \\1 \quad\quad 当 \dfrac{1}{2} \leq \sigma < 1 \end{cases} $$ 所以$(1) = (2)$命题得证

(e)

由前面知道，重点是要证明 $$ \lfloor N \cdot \sigma \rfloor = \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{N} \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{2}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} \rfloor . $$ 把$\ [0,1)\ $分成个$\ N\ $区间，$\ \sigma\ $落在$i\ $区间上 $\dfrac{i}{N} \leq \sigma < \dfrac{i+1}{N} (i \in Z, \ 0 \leq i < N) \tag{5} $ 所以 $$ \begin {split} i \leq N \cdot \sigma < i+1 \Rightarrow \lfloor N \cdot \sigma \rfloor = i \end {split} \tag{5.5} $$ 把$ \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{N} \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{2}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} \rfloor . $分成两部分 $$ \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{N} \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{2}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} \rfloor . \tag{6} $$ $$ \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} \rfloor . \tag{7} $$ 因为$ \sigma + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} < \dfrac{i+1}{N} + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} < 1 \Rightarrow \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} \rfloor = 0 $
由此推导$(6)$ $$ \begin {split} \lfloor \sigma \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{1}{N} \rfloor + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{2}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} \rfloor &< N \cdot \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i-1}{N} \rfloor = 0 \end {split} $$ 因为 $\sigma + \dfrac{N-i}{N} \geq \dfrac{i}{N} + \dfrac{N-i}{N} \geq 1 \Rightarrow \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i}{N} \rfloor = 1$
$\sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} < \dfrac{i+1}{N} + \dfrac{N-1}{N} < 2 \Rightarrow \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} \rfloor = 1$
由此推导$(7)$ $$ \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-i}{N} \rfloor + ... + \lfloor \sigma + \dfrac{N-1}{N} \rfloor = 1 + 1 + ... +1 = N-1-(N-i) + 1 = i $$ $(5.5) = (6)+(7)$命题得证
