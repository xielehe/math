证明

由 $\ p\ $ 整除 \(A^{2}-5\). \( \quad \Rightarrow\)
\(\exists \ c\) , \(pc=A^{2}-5\). \( \quad \Rightarrow\)
\(A^{2}\equiv 5\left( mod \ p\right)\). \( \quad \Rightarrow\)
$\ 5\ $是$\ p\ $的二次剩余. \( \quad \Rightarrow\)
\( \left( \dfrac {5}{p}\right) =1 \). \( \quad \left( 1\right) \)
根据二次互反律有\( \quad \Rightarrow\) \( \left( \dfrac {5}{p}\right) =\left( \dfrac {p}{5}\right) \). \( \quad \left( 2\right) \)
根据\( \ \left( 1\right) \) 和 \( \ \left( 2\right) \) 有 \( \left( \dfrac {p}{5}\right) =1 \). \( \quad \Rightarrow\)
\(p\equiv 1\left( mod \ 5\right)\ \)或\(\ \ p\equiv 4\left( mod \ 5\right)\)
